FAQs

  • How do I start a group game in Q&A?
    Choose “Groups”, enter player names, then start the round.
  • Are scores saved?
    Yes, the leaderboard is saved locally on your device.
  • Audio/fonts look wrong.
    Update iOS to the latest version, then restart the app.
